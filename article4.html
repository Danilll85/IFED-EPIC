New board game is coming — The Witcher: Path of Destiny!

We're happy to announce The Witcher: Path of Destiny — a new board game created in cooperation with Go On Board and Łukasz Woźniak, authors of critically acclaimed The Witcher: Old World.

Game Features

Path of Destiny is a tableau-building card game based on iconic tales from The Witcher universe. Players will be able to take on the roles of various characters from games and novels, including:

  • Geralt of Rivia
  • Ciri
  • Yennefer
  • Vesemir

Each character comes with unique cards and abilities. Clever hand management and strategic use of characters' skills will be key to victory.
